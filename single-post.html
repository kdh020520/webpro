Recommendation List - Avril Lavigne'

This list contains my personal opinion. So, Don't ever contradict!! I chose my five favorite songs, so listen to them yourself and decide whether they're good or not. Only Youtube music users can use play button, Sorry. Anyway, Here we go ~~

1. Runaway - The Best Damn Thing

This song is included in the 3rd track of The Best Damn Thing. If you like a song that is likely to appear in a teenage movie, I recommend it!


2. F.U. - Love Sux

This song is included in the 9th track of Love Sux. If you like Lavigne' songs, Just Listen!!


3. Boise Lie(feat. Machine Gun Kelly) - Love Sux

This song is included in the 2nd track of Love Sux. If you like Lavigne' songs, Just Listen!! (repeat)


4. Nobody's Home - Under My Skin

This song is included in the 7th track of Under My Skin. It's a gloomy song that only Lavigne can produce


5. Smile - Goodbye Lullaby

This songs is included in the 5th track of the Goodbye Lullaby. It's ambiguous to categorize it as a pop-punk, but this song has its own charm so, listen to it once
